What is JS Hacks?

JSHacks is an event dedicated to JavaScript developers and their communities.

The social coding experience is the central hub of the event and the main driver of collaboration and knowledge sharing while hands-on mentorship, workshops, trainings, talks and an event-ready development infrastructure provide the means.

Its aim is to foster the ever growing network of developers, bridge geographical sparse and niched communities, enable learning through knowledge sharing activities and finally empower participants to be social champions by tackling society's biggest issues through 48h coding challenges.

The first edition takes place on the 21-23 October, in two locations simultaneously: Bucharest/Romania & Chisinau/Moldova. Make friends, make products, make a difference ... and continue in doing so after the event!

OPEN DATA CHALLENGES

This edition's theme is “Open Data” and we're getting together to solve challenges such as:

  • Unifying the available data from data.gov.ro & data.gov.md
  • Using APIs for Transport/Infrastructure for Smart City solutions
  • Adopting/implementing APIs in the scope of local administration for public data
  • Access to anonymous medical data
  • Using APIs for notifications in emergency cases (earthquakes, floods, snow storms, etc.)

Have your own challenge you want to hack? Join us and let's build a solution together!
